UTWVC is a University of Texas at Austin RecSports affiliated volleyball club with the mission of winning national championships. We are a student run organization that is committed to excellence by providing great competition to all other universities across the nation. Our goal is to enhance the college experience of each member through volleyball engagement. We hire coaches from around the Austin area who are dedicated to helping us achieve a continued high level of competition.
